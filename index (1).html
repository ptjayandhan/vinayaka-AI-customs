⚠ The Problem

Manual Customs Is Broken

Cross-border shipments require correct HS classification, valuation, origin declarations, and dozens of supporting documents — all prepared manually from invoices and packing lists. This is slow, expensive, and error-prone.

  • Misclassification leading to fines and holds
  • Inconsistent data across fragmented documents
  • Unpredictable clearance times disrupting supply chains
  • Country-specific format complexity multiplying manual effort
  • No systematic compliance checks before submission
✦ Our Solution

AI That Clears the Way

ClearAI ingests raw shipment data — PDFs, spreadsheets, system exports — and automatically classifies, validates, and generates complete customs packages ready for electronic submission.

  • ML/NLP models trained on millions of historical entries
  • Confidence scores and explainability for every classification
  • Real-time sanctions, embargo, and threshold checks
  • Auto-generated declarations across country-specific formats
  • Human-in-the-loop review for complex or high-value shipments
How It Works

From Raw Documents to Cleared Shipment

A fully automated pipeline that handles the entire customs workflow — from data ingestion to electronic submission — with expert oversight at critical stages.

01
Ingest & Extract

Upload commercial invoices, packing lists, contracts, or product catalogs. AI extracts all key fields: descriptions, quantities, weights, values, Incoterms, parties, and country of origin.

02
Classify & Score

ML models propose HS codes and classification attributes for each line item, with confidence scores and explainability traces referencing similar past shipments.

03
Validate & Flag

Cross-check against OFAC sanctions lists, embargo rules, value thresholds, quantity limits, and country-specific licensing requirements. Flag risks before submission.

04
Generate & Submit

Auto-fill complete customs declarations and supporting documents in lane-specific formats. Transmit to brokers or electronic portals with full audit trails and version history.
